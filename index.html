We, the people of the Frankly project feel empowered to bring you the very best technology when it comes to converging the business jargon that has become ubiquitous in corporate communications. We have all heard the phrase “someone will be on the ground” and it begs the question “As opposed to in the sky?”

With that sub-par piece of humor in mind, we decided that this project gave us the bandwidth to uncover business buzz words that, all too often, litter the corporate landscape. "Let's talk turkey," "that's a rubber check," and other such gems can be nauseatingly overused but also a great source of humor! Whether it's the oddity of the phrase or that they fondly bring to mind "that guy at work who won't stop saying--", these buzzwords can be enjoyed and hated in equal measure!

With Frankly, users can enter a company's mission statement, a colleague's droll email, or another piece of corporate communication! We'll provide a count of how many buzz words were used and analyze of the text complexity and sentiment. We also take the big data approach by comparing the text to a list of our favorite buzzwords, just to make sure we circle back and provide you with a firm answer. You can even cover your bases by adding your favorite buzzwords.

Enjoy, play around, and poke fun at your co-workers!

-The Frankly Team
